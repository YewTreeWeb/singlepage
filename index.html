About

I have been developing now for 7 years and started learning coding back at Aberystwyth University in 2009. For CMS driven I always turn to WordPress and for ecommerce Shopify. However, I also have a passion for the static generator, with my go to being Jekyll. Which is what this site is built with!

YewTree Web is my online identity for web projects and web work. It first began at uni as mathewteagueyewtreeweb (mathew teague yewtree web) but people found it hard to say, so YewTree Web was born. My father had is own timber and damp, revenervation business called YewTree Renervations. After he past away the business was disolved so I wanted to bring back the name. Of course my line of business is very different so I adapted it to web.

I am currently the senior developer at a digital agency called Kubix Media where we primaly specialise in Shopify but do WordPress development as well. Which is where I come in. Having experience in both platforms allows me to switch and work on different projects at once, leaving the dedicated Shopify developer to handle the rest of the Shopify sites.
